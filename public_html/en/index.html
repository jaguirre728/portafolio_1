About me:

I was born in Yalí (Antioquia), I grew up in Envigado next to my paternal grandmother, I am currently 48 years old and I live in Bello municipality that is part of the metropolitan area of the Aburrá Valley. In 2008 I graduated as a Systems Engineer from the University of Antioquia Medellín Colombia.


Since September 2022 I have been working at the GFT company, in the position of Solution Consultant I, during this time I have been assigned to the support project of a highly demanded Core banking application in our country, I have had the opportunity to be part of a large interdisciplinary group that day by day we overcome the challenges involved in maintaining and guaranteeing 7/24 operation, having the pride of seeing daily transactions grow 5x, carrying out development work on the backend to guarantee timely and clear monitoring through the visibility of metrics that help us have constant supervision through state-of-the-art tools such as Dynatrace, attending to the requirements of internal clients through SQL queries to the Oracle database is a fundamental part of the area in which I find myself, which is CoreOps..



Before joining GFT, I worked at Soluciones Latinas SAS for around 13 years, serving as a support engineer. My responsibilities included addressing the requirements presented by the various clients in charge of our incident management platform..


During my work, I developed extensive knowledge in structuring SQL queries in the Postgres database engine, analyzing the software developed in PHP to define which modules needed to be intervened by the developers in order to resolve reported incidents, perform testing in pre-production environments and their corresponding deployment to production..


I consider myself a proactive person, always with the attitude to learn and improve every day, with teamwork skills.


My medium-term goal is to strengthen my knowledge of AWS, as we are currently migrating the application we support to the cloud. That's why I'm taking the AWS Cloud Practitioner course with the goal of becoming certified in the next three months.
